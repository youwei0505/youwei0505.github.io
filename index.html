Biography

My name is You-Wei Jiang (蔣有為 in Chinese). I am a Master’s degree student in the Digital IC Chip Lab at National Cheng Kung University in Tainan, Taiwan. The lab is currently led by Distinguished Prof. Pei-Yin Chen.

My research mainly focuses on machine learning and image processing technologies.

My Master’s thesis is mainly dedicated to “MPEG-7 CDVS”, a technology used for image matching and recognition, including scene recognition and object recognition.
